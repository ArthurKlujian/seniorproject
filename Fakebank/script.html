Features

Operations

No waiting in lines just petting your favorite animal!

Any animal (that is safe) we can get our hands on harm-free. These animals will be saved not bred or captured. You are helping to prevent poaching and unnecessary breeding.

We hold opportunities to provide homes to certain animals

Many animals we rescue and rehabilitate need homes! We house many events for donations or to create homes for these animals. Of course the best way is to keep their natural homes clean and healthy!

No longer in need to pet an animal? No problem!

We understand if plans change or if you no longer want to pet animals. While we would never want to stop we get it. It is super easy to cancel your membership and just as easy to come back to us!
